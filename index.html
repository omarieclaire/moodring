3 questions
for you,
seeker

One:
are you light as air or rooted in the earth?

Two:
will you flow out or will you peer in?

Two:
do you pull the deep flowing dark or follow the light floating joy?

Three:
do you spring into the morning or sink into the pulsing night?

Three:
do we slowly unfurl or loop in endless return?

Three:
will you leap into the chaos or undulate into the calm?

Three:
will you wander into a forest or twirl through a meadow?
